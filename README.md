# Matthew Green's Professional Site

Hello, this is my professional site. I have developed this site to showcase my skills I learned from recent courses in CMPA. This site includes links to my work, a slider on the homepage, and interactive features such as an embedded google search. I also invite anyone to check my site out and offer feedback.

## Purpose

I created this professional site to showcase my skills to employers. It is a way to keep a collection of the work I have completed so far. I also invite anyone to take a look at my work to offer feedback and maybe even take away some ideas from my site. 

## Changes from Project 01 to Project 02

Project 01 offered the functional layout of my website. It was lacking clear wording on the pages and the homepage needed more content. I also had multiple contact sections on the contact page. For Project 02, I cleaned up my wording. I decided to embed a google search option on the homepage to make it easy for someone visiting my site to pull up a quick search without leaving the page. I chose to add windy to the homepage as well to give my targeted audience a view of the weather where I am located. I changed the slider images to images of words in order of how to plan a project. The old images of the homepages of sites I had did not seem to fit in, because these homepages already have links on my project page.
